Top three activities to do at Lagos

Visit Ikeja City Mall

Ever wondered how it felt to shop in heaven? Ikeja city mall is the closest thing you will get

Take a stroll to Freedom Park

Freedom Park is a memorial and leisure park area in the middle of downtown Lagos in Lagos Island

Ignite the artist in you at Nike Art gallery

Located in Lekki, Lagos, the four-story building compromises of an art gallery and textile museum featuring over 7,000 pieces and soon to have Coffee shop
